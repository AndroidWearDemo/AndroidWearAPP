Get Pomodoro on your wrist
The app provides 25 minute working cycles and 5 minute cycles to rest. By vibration on your wrist, you will know when a cycle is over.
If you want to run the app on a smartwatch you will need an Android Wear Device such as the LG G Watch, Samsung Gear Live or the Moto 360.
By using this simple time management method, you will be able to work more focused. The idea is also, that frequent breaks can improve your mental agility. Find more information about the Pomodoro technique: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pomodoro_Technique
