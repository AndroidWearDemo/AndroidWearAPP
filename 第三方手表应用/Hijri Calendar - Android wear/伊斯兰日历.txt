Hijri Calender it is a small app for android wear to display The Islamic Calender.
This app is the first Hijri Calender for android wear in play store.
This app it`s working only on android wear device
Main Features:
1. Display Hijri and Gregorian Date.
2. English and Arabic month names .
3. Offset Days "IF the Hijri Date are not correct".
