A clone of the famous Flappy Bird genre of games but with an added advantage. This loveable game is now compatible with your android wear devices such as LG G Watch, Samsung Gear Live and Moto 360.
An easy enough to play game on your Android Wear device. A tap makes the bird fly and not tapping makes it fall . So tap away on your favourite Android Wear to this addictive game which is one of the first game released on the Android Wear.
Just install this game on your phone and the Android Wear will push it to the appropriate Android Wear device connected to the phone via bluetooth.
