Turn your Android Wear into Torch. It's useful, simple and fast. Very convenient when you urgently need some light in the dark. To start the app, Just say: OK Google... Start Torch or Open Torch.
This app is compatible with LG G Watch, Samsung Gear Live, Asus Zenwatch, Sony Smartwatch 3 and Moto 360
Please note:
-Make sure your mobile is compatible with Android Wear.
-Make sure to pair your phone with the wearable device with help of the Android Wear App.
-To open: Go to "Start..." and open the app "Torch" or say "Open Torch"
